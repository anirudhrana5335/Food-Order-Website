We have stepped up standard hygiene, cleanliness processes at our outlets.

We have recently restarted home delivery from select restaurants in Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Hissar, Dehradun and Amritsar. The move comes in view of the government’s directive that categorised food delivery as one of the essential services. It is noteworthy that following the government’s announcement of the lockdown, the American burger chain had shut down over 150 restaurants in the north and eastern region.

With safety of the customers and employees as the topmost priority, we have stepped up the standard hygiene and cleanliness processes by introducing a number of additional measures. These actions are in line with McDonald’s core values of quality, service and cleanliness, which become even more relevant in these challenging times. We have made hand sanitizers available for everyone’s use at the restaurants. Further, we have enhanced our sanitation protocols by introducing broad spectrum hospital-grade sanitizers to disinfect all frequently touched points/surfaces in the restaurants.
